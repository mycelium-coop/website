<!-- @id: service-1-title -->
Technical Assistance

<!-- @id: service-1-body -->
We help cooperatives and aligned organizations navigate the full lifecycle of their technology needs. This includes troubleshooting issues in existing platforms and interfacing with tech support on our partners' behalf; evaluating current systems and workflows to identify opportunities for improvement and streamlining; and integrating workflows across multiple platforms to reduce fragmented, manual processes. When new tools are needed, we research and recommend platforms tailored to specific needs, and support our partners in vetting software vendors—ensuring they get solutions that genuinely serve them at the best possible price. We also help with onboarding staff onto new systems. For organizations looking to move away from Big Tech platforms, we guide the transition to open-source or values-aligned alternatives where suitable options exist.

<!-- @id: service-2-title -->
Custom Software Solutions

<!-- @id: service-2-body -->
Sometimes the right tool for the job doesn't exist yet. When our partners have needs that aren't well served by off-the-shelf platforms, we work alongside them to collaboratively design and build custom software from the ground up. Our process is deeply relational—we take the time to get to know the people we're working with and understand the real context and constraints of each project, so that what we build is anchored in our partners' actual needs, not assumptions. This could look like a governance tool for cooperative decision-making, a member-facing platform, or a workflow application tailored to a specific operation—whatever the need calls for.
